Classic Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

A burger is a patty of ground beef grilled and placed between two halves of a bun. Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor. Burgers are considered an American food but are popular around the world.

Ingredients

    For the Patties:

  • ! pound ground beef(80% lean)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

    • For the Burger:

  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheese slices (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices (raw or caramelized, as preferred)
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Instruction

Step 1: Prepare the Patties

Divide the ground beef into equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Season each patty with salt and pepper on both sides.

Step 2: Cook the Patties

Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the burger patties for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to allow it to melt.

Step 3: Toast the Buns

While the patties are cooking, cut the hamburger buns in half and toast them on the grill or in a toaster until golden brown.

Step 4: Assemble the Burger

Spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun. Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom bun, followed by the cooked burger patty with melted cheese. Add tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles on top.

Step 5: Sauce it Up

Squirt ketchup and mustard on the top bun or customize with your favorite sauces.

Step 6: Put it Together

Place the top bun over the assembled ingredients, creating a delicious and classic cheeseburger.

Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

Serve your cheeseburgers hot and enjoy this classic American favorite with your favorite side dishes.
