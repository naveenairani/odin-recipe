Delicious Momos Recipe

Description

Momos are a popular Asian dish, consisting of steamed dumplings filled with a mixture of meat and/or vegetables. Served with a flavorful dipping sauce, momos make for a delightful and comforting snack or meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground meat (chicken, pork, or a combination)
  • Finely chopped vegetables (cabbage, carrots, and spring onions)
  • Garlic and ginger, minced
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Wonton or dumpling wrappers
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • For the Dipping Sauce:
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Chili sauce or chili flakes

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Filling

Mix ground meat, chopped vegetables, minced garlic, minced ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl.

Step 2: Assemble the Momos

Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of a wonton wrapper. Fold the wrapper in half to create a half-moon shape, sealing the edges with water. Repeat for the remaining wrappers and filling.

Step 3: Steam the Momos

Arrange the momos in a steamer and steam for about 12-15 minutes or until the wrappers are cooked through.

Step 4: Prepare the Dipping Sauce

Mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and chili sauce or chili flakes to create a flavorful dipping sauce.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Serve the steamed momos hot with the dipping sauce on the side. Enjoy this delightful Asian treat!
